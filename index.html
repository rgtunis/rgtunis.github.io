My experience

Withings | Design Intern | Summer 2023

I was so fortunate to intern for Withings in their office just outside Paris, France. While Withings creates a variety of innovative health monitoring technologies, my focus was on the design and content of features within the app, specifically for users receiving alerts about heart irregularities. In addition to contributing to the design and flow of several heart health functions, I independently created a three-week educational program aimed at Withings users with atrial fibrillation.

FasterCures, a Center of the Milken Institute | Senior Associate | 2018-2021

I worked at FasterCures, a Center of the Milken Institute for three years on the Health & Technology team. My research explored a variety of issues related to the collection and use of health data in the context of biomedical research, such as bias in health datasets, regulatory gaps in health data protections, and the interoperability of disparate health data systems and sources.
